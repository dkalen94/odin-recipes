Spanish Tortilla

Description

This is the best recipe for making delicious tortillas.

Tortilla is a typical dish from Spain. It contains potatoes, sometimes onions and eggs.

Ingredients

Depending on how many people you are cooking for, you will need more or less of the following ingredients: (for 2 people)

  • 8 Potatoes
  • 4 Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the potatoes.
  2. Heat the olive oil mid temperature in a pan.
  3. Add the sliced potatoes and cook until you can break them with a fork.
  4. Add the potatoes in a bowl and let them cool a bit
  5. Beat the eggs and mix them with the potatoes.
  6. Season with salt and cook until set.
  7. Add the mixed ingredients to a pan and cook each side until golden.
