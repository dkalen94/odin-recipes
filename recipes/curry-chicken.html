Curry Chicken Recipe

Description

This is the recipe from an indian restaurant.

Curry chicken is a popular dish in Indian cuisine, known for its rich and aromatic flavors.

Ingredients

  • 500 gr chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. chop the garlic and onion
  2. heat the oil
  3. cook the chicken until golden
  4. add the garlic and onion to the pan and stir until it gets a brown color
  5. add tomato puree
  6. stir until all is mixed
  7. add the coconut milk
  8. add the spices and stir until all is mixed and ready to serve with the rice
