Caesar Salad

Caesar salad is one of the tastiest salads in the world

It is easy and fast to prepare

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Chicken
  • Bread cubes
  • Caesar sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. wasch the lettuce
  2. cut the chicken into strips
  3. heat the oil
  4. cook the chicken until golden
  5. season the chicken with salt and pepper
  6. mix the lettuce with chicken, bread cubes and add the sauce
  7. enjoy it
